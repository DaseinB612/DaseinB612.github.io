## ⚖️ TrolleyBench
An ethics-first alignment benchmark that stress-tests alignment proposals against 2,500 years of moral philosophy. Identifies where proposals align with established ethical thought, where they contradict it, and where they fall into known philosophical traps.

[View on GitHub](https://github.com/DaseinB612/trolleybench)
